Here's a list of useful links and references related to computer ethics:

  • BCS Code of Conduct - The British Computer Society's code of conduct is based on four principles and serves as a code of ethics for IT professionals.
  • EU Data Protection Laws - The European Union's data protection laws include the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), which strengthens two fundamental rights in the digital age: the right to the protection of personal data concerning the self, and the right to access and rectify collected data concerning the self.
  • IEEE-CS Code of Ethics - The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers' Computer Society's code of ethics is based on eight principles for software engineers to adhere to.
  • Moor, J H. (1985) What is computer ethics?. Metaphilosophy 16(4): 266-275. - Moor's derivation of the scope and role of computer ethics is both critical and impressive, where he draws upon an interpretation of history, and three types of semblances related to computers.
