GDPR


Here's a list of useful links and references related to GDPR:

  • Official GDPR PDF
  • Renaud, K. & Shepherd, L. (2018) 'How to Make Privacy Policies both GDPR-Compliant and Usable'. 2018 International Conference On Cyber Situational Awareness, Data Analytics And Assessment (Cyber SA). Glasgow, UK, 2018. 1-8. DOI. - Renaud outline the creation of a privacy policy that's GDPR compliant.
  • CIPL. (2018) 'The Case for Accountability: How it Enables Effective Data Protection and Trust in the Digital Society'. Available from: here. - CIPR present a guide to accountability, including an accountability model.
  • Hamon, R., Junklewitz, H., Sanchez, I., Malgieri, G. & Hert, P. (2022) Bridging the Gap Between AI and Explainability in the GDPR: Towards Trustworthiness-by-Design in Automated Decision-Making. IEEE Computational Intelligence Magazine. 17(1): 72-85. DOI. - Hamon et al explore the integration of XAI and Automated Decision Making.
  • Brodin, M. (2019) 'A Framework for GDPR Compliance for Small- and Medium-Sized Enterprises'. Available from: here. - Brodin present a guide to GDPR compliance for SMEs (small and medium sized enterprises).
  • BAB. (2019) 'How to comply with GDPR '. Available from: here. - BAB present a brief guide for complying with GDPR.
